LearnVectors

LearnVectors is a E-Learning platform, that aims at providing educational content to the tier 3 students in India.

About the project

LearnVectors is a E-Learning platform, that aims at providing educational content to the tier 3 students in India. Their content is focused on the students apiring to prepare for the competetive examinations in the fields of engineering, medical and completion of their primary higher education. After the pandemic there has been a sharp increase in online education, we have seen alot of companies invest and increase their online presence. Although there have been alot of new companies coming up, most of these companies target the tier 1 and tier 2 students. By targeting tier 3 students LearnVectors learns its niche. As per the statistics prier to 2020, at an average 7 lakh students appear for there examinations and 35% of these students come from tier 3. In order to make it affordable for the students/families the content availabe to them is broken down till specific chapters can also be purchased. To increase the reach to the target audience we also provide them with limited free data to get acustomed and used to the platform. It also includes previous year paper, doubt solving session and animated video content with experienced teachers explaining concepts.

I was bought onto the project to consult on the interface that the team had initially developed on their own as best as they could with their knowledge of aesthetics and usability, but as this was done more intuitively rather than data based. I provided the with clearer road map and organised data driven structure to Redesign the application. I spent alot of time speaking with and understadning the vision of the company its USPâ€™s, product offerings etc before i could plan ahead. I applied principles of Usability Principles and Heuristic Analysis, in order to begin my Design Process. Pain points like : Navigation, Identification of courses, Understanding and prioritising tasks, Decreasing confusion and visual overload , were my major problems areas.

In Retrospect

LearnVector was a completely different project as compared to Amplichain, even the domains for the two were completely different. Moving from a diagnostic field to the field of education. Although learning from the latter helped me alot. I got the opportunity of working on Learnvectors due to my performance on Amplichain. With a new project there were newer challenges and as a designer I was ready to face them all. I was brought onto the project as a consultant for the user experience design of the product, they already had created a base design and were in the development phase when I was involved. My first challenge was to do a thorough analysis of the recent design and solve the problems arising in it and to fix them in the most aesthetic and least time consuming way. Due to the style of the product and the amount of information it held it was important to segregate all the data into different streams. It was utmost necessary to create user flows that were less straining to the cognitive load of the user and also had the least learning curve. Once again due to team effort I was able to complete this project and is currently available in the android play store.
