About the project

Biosense Technologies Pvt Ltd, develops diagnostic medical devices. QPCR is a diagnostic medical devices. It is a machine that is used to detect different viruses present in a human body. This machine is used by diagonostic labs and blood banks to do their respective tests. Even though the internal system is 100% accurate they still required a Usable and User-Friendly interaction with the machine in order to achieve the tasks with ease and accuracy. QPCR is in high demand due to the pademic situaton experienced, hence it was atmost importance for the machine to be extremely user-friendly.

As an designer i was tasked with creating the user flow and the visual design of the machine to achieve ease of use and accuracy. As per the marketing team a single machine was to cater to two different user groups ie the Diagnostic labs and the Blood banks to decrease cost of production and increase their market segment. All thought the tests to be done remain the same the process and frequeny of the test at both the settlements are different, hence i had design the User - Flow in a modular yet simple way to give the best user experience to any kinf of user.
